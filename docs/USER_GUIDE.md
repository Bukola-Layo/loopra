# Loopra User Guide

Welcome to Loopra, the easiest way to grow and automate audience communication.

## 1. Getting Started
Once you log into your dashboard, the **Overview** page will show your total subscribers, active loops, and recent campaigns.
To start growing your audience, you need to set up a **Form**.

## 2. Managing Your Audience
- Navigate to **Audience > Subscribers** to view your entire list.
- You can manually add subscribers or import them via CSV.
- **Segments** allow you to filter your subscribers by tags, creation date, and interaction history.

## 3. Creating Forms
1. Go to **Audience > Forms**.
2. Click **Create Form**.
3. Customize the fields (Email is required; First/Last Name are optional).
4. Save the form and copy the Embed Code or the Hosted URL to share with your audience.

## 4. Campaigns
Campaigns are one-off email blasts sent to your audience or a specific segment.
1. Navigate to **Campaigns**.
2. Click **New Campaign**.
3. Use the Drag-and-Drop editor or AI Content Generator to design your email.
4. Select your target audience.
5. Send immediately or schedule for later.

## 5. Automation Loops
Loops are automated workflows triggered by user actions.
1. Navigate to **Loops**.
2. Click **Create Loop**.
3. Select a **Trigger** (e.g., "Subscriber signs up via Form A").
4. Add **Actions** (e.g., "Send Welcome Email", "Add Tag: VIP", "Wait 3 Days").
5. Turn the Loop **ON** to start automating your workflow!
